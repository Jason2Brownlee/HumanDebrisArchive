One Shot Kills

Modification: One Shot Kills
Group: Human Debris
Modification Type: Client/Server
Quake III Arena Version: Q3A 1.27g
Source Code Included: Yes
Description: OSK stands for One Shot Kills. OSK:Arena is basically a mod that is based on Unreal Tournaments Instagib style of game play. The modification actually includes TEN different versions of instagib, selectable via a menu system. The hope of the mod is to keep the game play fast, and fun.

In Depth:
OSK:Arena includes ten different versions of instagib:

  1. Classic Instagib (UT Style)
    This is an exact copy of Unreal Tournament's Instagib style of game play. All players spawn into a level with a railgun and unlimited ammunition. There is no ammunition or weapons at all, in any maps. The projectile is instantaneous. The projectile is impossible to dodge. As soon as a player is hit with a projectile, they gib instantly, and a frag is awarded.

  2. Rifle Instagib
    This is the same as the above 'Classic Instagib'. The difference is that the projectile is a bullet. This is for all those players that are sick of using 'fictional weapons', and want a bit more lead in their diet.

  3. Telefrag Instagib (Telefrag Arena)
    The biggest complaint of instagib is that players can camp in a populated area and clock up frag's. Telefrag Instagib tries to eliminate camping. Telefrag Arena follows the same rules as 'Classic Instagib'. Instead of instantly gibbing, players teleport over the top of other players. Because two players cannot be in the same place at once, targeted players are "Telefragged". Telefragging was a common occurrence in the original quake, it has been missed in Quake2 and Quake3. Although Telefragging does occur in Q2 and Q3:A, it is not as frequent as it was in Quake. This style of instagib aims to share the joy of a good telefrag!

  4. Bouncing Instagib
    This style is the same as 'Classic Instagib', with a few small changes. The projectile is not instantaneous, but rather a fast moving sphere. The sphere can be bounced off walls. This is useful for fragging opponents around corners, or bouncing projectiles at player off walls or the floor.

  5. Gauntlet Instagib
    All players spawn with the gauntlet. To get a frag you have to run right up to players and hit them. If you like to camp, you will not get many frag's with this style of gameplay.

  6. Delayed Instagib (Classic)
    Delayed Instagib follows the same rules as "Classic Instagib". The difference is that players gib or explode five seconds after they have been shot. Once a player is shot, they glow. If a player dies or uses the 'kill' command before the two seconds is up, the player looses a frag and no frag is awarded.
    This is the cool bit! If a player gets shot a second time the explode instantly in a shower of gibs. This means if you shoot a player you are not guaranteed a frag. Some other player can come along and shoot the victim for the second time, and get the frag.

  7. Delayed Instagib (Jump 'n Gib)
    Delayed Instagib follows the same rules as "Classic Instagib". When a player is shot they become immune to all weapons fire for 1 second. The catch here is that the player is thrust straight up into the air making it difficult to aim, and providing a nice shower of gibs when their time is up :) When hit, a player will be shot into the air, and stay up there. In that one second before death, players can move around in the air similar the the flight rune.

  8. Delayed Instagib (Invisible)
    This style of instagib is the same as the above 'Delayed Instagib (Classic)'. The difference is that instead of glowing when you have been shot you become invisible. This changes the game so that getting shot is not all that bad. The player becomes invisible and can run around shooting others for three seconds until they explode.

  9. Delayed Instagib (Haste)
    See a trend here? Same as the above, instead of becoming invisible, a hit player is given the haste rune for five seconds. Run rings around your attacker before you explode, unless you get shot a second time...

  10. Delayed Instagib (Blast)
    This is another take on the Delayed Instagib game style, but this one packs a punch!
    This game style follows the same rules as the above 'Delayed Instagib (Classic)'. This time the targeted player becomes a time bomb on legs! Five seconds after been hit, the player will explode big. Any players too close will be killed by the blast, and frags awarded to the player that blew up. If a player that has already been hit, is hit a second time... BANG!
    This style turns players into kamikaze's who can get some revenge on the player that shot them, maybe...
    The blast will only kill players that have not been hit (unprotected). Players that have been hit (started their countdown), will be unhurt, but will be blasted away by the shock wave. This makes for some very funny in game situations!!!


Changing the version of Instagib :
The type of instagib that you play is chosen from the menu. A new sub menu has been added to the main menu. Simply go into the new "OSK Menu" and select which style of instagib you want to play.
The style of instagib can also be changed via the in-game menu (press escape while in a level). Select "OSK Menu" from the ingame menu.


Works With:
Deathmath: Yes

Capture the Flag: Yes
Bots: Yes


Installation / Running:

  1. Unzip the archive into your quake3:arena directory.
  2. Load quake3 normally
  3. Select the "One Shot Kills" modification from the "Mods" menu.
  4. Load up a map and test it out.


Special Notes:
This is a client/server modification, which means if a server is running this modification the client needs to download the modification as well.
